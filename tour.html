Details
Day 1 : Arrival in Jaisalmer
  • On arrival, our representative will pick you at Jaisalmer Railway Station/Airport.
  • Ther driver will drop you at the hotel, where you can rest till 2-3pm .
  • Later with the travel guide you'll visit the beautiful sites of Vyas Chhatri and Gadisagar Lake and etc.
Day 2 : Fort and desert tour
  • After breakfast at the hotel, you will enjoy a half day Jaisalmer Fort tour, where you will visit: Patwon haveli, Salim Singh haveli, Jain temples and many 300-600 years old mansion.
  • After lunch, you'll check out from the hotel and we will reach Sam Sand Dunes which is 40km from Jaisalmer.
  • You will arrive at the booked desert camp by 4pm, where you will be received with a traditional welcome and Tikka Dasturi by camp staff.
  • Later you will enjoy a desert safari tour (camel safari) and also see the desert sunset point.
  • From 7:30pm you will enjoy a cultural musical program with buffet dinner and later rest in your camp.
Day 3 : Explore Bada Bagh, Museum and local markets
  • After the morning breakfast at the desert camp, you will check out from the campsite by 9-10am.
  • Later you'll visit Bada Bagh, Jaisalmer War museum, or an optional tour of Kuldhara Haunted Village.
  • After lunch, enjoy a free walk at the local markets and later the driver will drop at train station or airport.
